Poop Report is an app I made for my dear friend Brandy. Living in a multi-generational household and caring for her parents' dogs with varying health issues, Brandy needed a way for the family to keep track of when the dogs had last been walked, if they had taken their prescriptions, eaten...


and a simple way to answer the all-important question:

MVP

"an app to prevent poopcidents"


To start with, Brandy wanted to have a place to record the following for each dog:

  1. Did they get outside to walk?
  2. Did they pee?
  3. Did they poop?
  4. Were they fed?
  5. Did they eat the food?
  6. Did they get their meds?

Additional options:

  • write a comment with each report
  • add a "profile photo" for each dog
  • upload a photo of the poop! (to help diagnose digestive troubles)
